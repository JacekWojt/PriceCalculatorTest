Price Calculator Test App is an application for testing
methods that calculate the price of purchases with discount coupons and categories.

The tests check whether
- the total price is zero if there are no products,
- the total price is equal to the price of the product without coupons,
- the total price is equal to the price of the product with coupons,
- the total price is equal to the price of the products and one coupon without categories,
- total price is equal to the price of the products and two coupons with category,
- the total price is equal to the price of the products and three coupons with category.
